Recipe of the Day





Ingredients:

- For the rice:
- 2 cups basmati rice
- 4 cups water
- 1 tablespoon oil or ghee
- Salt to taste
- 1 cinnamon stick
- 2-3 cardamom pods

- For the meat:
- 500g lamb or chicken (cut into pieces)
- 1 onion (chopped)
- 2 tablespoons oil or ghee
- 2 garlic cloves (minced)
- 1 teaspoon ground cumin
- 1 teaspoon ground coriander
- Salt to taste
- 2 tomatoes (chopped)
- 2 cups water or broth

- For the topping:
- 2 large carrots (cut into strips)
- 1/2 cup raisins
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- 1 tablespoon oil or ghee

Instructions:

1. Cook the rice:
- Rinse the rice and soak it for 30 minutes.
- Boil 4 cups of water with salt, cinnamon, and cardamom.
- Add the soaked rice and cook for 10-12 minutes (don't fully cook the rice). Drain it.

2. Cook the meat:
- Heat oil in a pot, cook the onions until golden, then add garlic, cumin, coriander, and salt.
- Add the meat and brown it. Then add tomatoes and water or broth.
- Cook until the meat is tender (about 20 minutes in a pressure cooker or 30-40 minutes in a regular pot).

3. Prepare the topping:
- Heat oil in a pan and cook the carrots for 5 minutes.

- Add raisins, sugar, and cook for a few more minutes until the raisins puff up.

4. Assemble the Qabuli Pulao:
- In a large pot, layer the cooked meat and rice.
- Cover the pot and cook on low heat for 10-15 minutes.

5. Serve:
- Fluff the rice, and top with the carrot, raisin, and nut mixture.

Enjoy your Qabuli Pulao!
